COMPANY PROFILE

DOBAL REAL ESTATE MANAGEMENT is a licensed survey and development services specializing in land survey, engineering survey and construction survey throughout the province of Tarlac. The firm was established in 2021 and has since developed rapidly growing clients and sound reputation in the industry which has resulted to be one of the top surveying firms to have high volume approved survey submittals in the province.

OUR PEOPLE

We have a professional and dedicated team of Surveyors, Draftsmen, Survey Technicians and Office staffs that have a combined experience in the industry throughout the province. Our expertise allows us to cater from small to large scale land survey services.

OUR EQUIPMENT

We continue to invest in the latest technology to provide accurate data for all projects. Our Nikon Total Station, South Total Station and ComNav GPS systems that enables our team with the upmost accuracy and confidence when locating data in the field. We also utilise a range of quality software products and databases including AutoCAD, and Civil 3D to locate information and produce quality plans in a range of formats for clients. We create written fee proposals for all projects and provide a prompt, efficient and reliable service that keeps our clients coming back.
