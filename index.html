ABOUT

Owner

Mazel L. Sahidsahid "Mazel", was born in Tagbilaran City, Bohol, Philippines, a Licensed Geodetic Engineer with ardent interest in Land Surveying, Construction, Engineering and Technology. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Geodetic Engineering degree at University of Bohol in Tagbilaran City. After her oath taking as a Licensed Geodetic Engineer, she established her own firm: DOBAL REAL STATE CORPORATION.

Survey Firm

DOBAL REAL ESTATE CORPORATION is a licensed survey and development services specializing in land survey, engineering survey and construction survey throughout the province of Tarlac. The firm was established in 2021 and has since developed rapidly growing clients and sound reputation in the industry which has resulted to be one of the top surveying firms to have high volume approved survey submittals in the province.
