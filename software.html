AutoDesk AutoCAD

Autodesk AutoCAD, often simply referred to as AutoCAD, is a computer-aided design (CAD) software application developed and marketed by Autodesk, Inc. It is one of the most widely used CAD programs globally and has been an industry standard for many years. AutoCAD allows users to create and edit 2D and 3D drawings and models with precision and efficiency.
